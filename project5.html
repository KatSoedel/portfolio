GlowCo: Customer Survey & Digital Behavior Study

2024

Overview

This project analyzed GlowCo’s customer survey and digital behavior data to uncover gaps in brand messaging, product perception, and channel performance. Our goal: turn insights into clear, actionable marketing strategies.

Draw Insights

Insight 1: Brand Value Disconnect — Only 42% of customers identified GlowCo’s mission; 29% confused the brand with a low-cost competitor. We recommended refreshing mission-driven messaging across campaigns and reinforcing brand identity in digital and customer touchpoints.
