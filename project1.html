AI/Tech Startup Marketing

2025

Overview

This pioneering AI startup is committed to reshaping the relationship between artificial intelligence and the classroom. Designed for both educators and students, the technology enables the ethical integration of AI tools in academic environments. By encouraging transparency, originality, and accountability, the platform empowers students to create their own work., as well as provide educators with powerful tools to monitor misuse and prevent AI-driven cheating. As the lead brand designer for this AI startup, I developed a full visual identity system from the ground up, aligning every brand touchpoint with its mission of honesty, innovation, and academic integrity.

Market Research

To develop a brand that resonates with both educators and students, I conducted in-depth market research that included: Competitor analysis of AI education tools Industry insights from edtech reports and academic journals The key insight: educators were seeking AI platforms that offered visibility and ethical safeguards, while students wanted tools that complemented, rather than replaced, their efforts.
